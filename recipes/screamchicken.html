Chicken and Sour Cream Sauce
Ingredients:
8 chicken breasts (boneless)
8 slices bacon
4 ounces dried beef		 16 ounces sour cream
1/2 tsp salt
2 cans Cream of Mushroom soup

Blend sour cream and soup. Line bottom of pan with about half of this mixture. Layer with dried beef. Wrap each breast with bacon. Cover with remaining sauce.

Don't add any seasoning!

Bake at 300° F for 3 hours.

From "Some Favorite Recipes" of Scott Siddall, http://siddall.info
