Outcomes and assessment

Expected Outcomes

  • Improved pedagogy and extended faculty expertise
  • Collaborative projects within and among classes
  • More effective course and curricululm structure
  • New course materials and resources
  • New methods of cost-containment
  • Expanded collaboration among and within institutions

Assessment

Both survey-based quantitative information as well as subjective data
  • How have teaching and learning been enhanced?

  • Has technology enhanced intercampus collaboration?
