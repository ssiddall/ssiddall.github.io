Collaborative projects

Competitively funded proposals from Denison and Kenyon faculty

Guidelines:

$500 to $15,000 for nearly anything but equipment

involve faculty and students from both campuses (some exceptions)

produce pedagogical methods that can be disseminated, used elsewhere

positive impact on learning, transferrable to other disciplines

cost-effective

Expect to fund 20-40 such projects in the three years of the project.

Some examples (only!) were submitted with the proposal:

  • Environmental studies
  • Mathematics
  • Public Policy and Philosophy
  • International studies
