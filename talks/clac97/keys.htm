Keys to Success



  • work together early to develop collaboration
  • browse - browse - browse, and pick models that work
  • define goals - audience, subject, coverage
  • design a "look and feel" guide for project unity
  • be clear about ownership issues
  • balance sizzle and substance
  • maximize interactivity - thoughtful links that gently guide
  • faculty takes responsibility for part of the site
  • divide labor - content for all, but assign technical, editorial, media, etc.
  • allocate ample time for review, revision, linking
  • caution with high maintenance elements - email, forms, dated references
