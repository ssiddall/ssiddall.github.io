Define Liaison

    "a person who initiates and maintains a contact or connection to ensure concerted action and cooperation"

Support services

    • Training
    • Consulting
    • Documentation

Expectations of good support

    • Effective communication - caution with jargon, style
    • Client's needs are primary - follow their lead, then suggest alternatives
    • Most valuable asset? mutual respect
    • Approach problems slowly each time
    • Just-in-Time support practices - and alternatives
    • Assume nothing about client, about problem
    • Consistent follow-through - nothing drops through the cracks
    • Follow-up on open-ended needs

Procedures

    Establish a schedule, report it, adhere to it
    Over your head in time, technology? Call for help!
    Maintain journal and report in each Friday
    Monthly summaries to department faculty
    Rely on HelpLine for routine support
    Know ICS staff and support procedures
    New resources needed? Know who to call
    Observe or survey faculty goals and uses of IT
    Routine support comes first - projects are ALWAYS secondary
    Report conflicts

    Write new documentation
