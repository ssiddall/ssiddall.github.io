Why Analyze Environmental Samples?
u What is the purpose of environmental sampling and analysis?
l To obtain a small but informative portion of the population being investigated
n You can’t sample the whole environment.
n There are an  infinite number of potential samples.
n Therefore, representative (or unrepresentative)       samples are taken and conclusions about the site are made based on these selected samples.
