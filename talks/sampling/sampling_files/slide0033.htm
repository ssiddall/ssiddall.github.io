Step 7 - Optimize the Design for Obtaining Data
u A satisfactory design for obtaining data to meet your specific objectives is rarely achieved the first time.
l Iteration is usually needed.
n If not with Steps 1 - 5, usually at least Step 6.
n Almost always iteration is needed with the numbers of samples to be analyzed because that affects the statistical confidence levels of the data and confidence may have to be decreased to meet budget limitations.
