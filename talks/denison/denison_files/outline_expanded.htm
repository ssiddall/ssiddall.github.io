These are NOT binary choices!
Books OR computers? - NO, both
Faculty OR technology? - NO, both
Face-to-face conversations OR e-mail? - NO, both

Bottom Line?
  The most significant benefits of information technology are not likely to be achieved without changes to our teaching
  Commitment to change is needed if learning technology is to enhance and sustain the characteristics of our institutions we value most
