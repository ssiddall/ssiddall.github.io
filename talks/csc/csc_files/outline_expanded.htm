Enhances information access
Increases student motivation, engagement

Learning Goals
to be challenged by information resources
to learn to overcome infoglut, to review and select resources carefully
to receive context from the instructor
to learn socially sometimes, independently others, through diverse means
to publish for audiences other than the instructor
to learn to communicate with electronic tools
to learn how to collaborate, avoid the pitfalls of moral over critical thinking

Challenges to success
breaking out of linear thinking
giving up control over users’ approaches to material
overcoming reliance on text
balancing sizzle and substance
maintaining a site over time
developing referencing strategies
scalable to larger classes, survey courses?

Keys to success
Browse, browse, browse!
Work together early to develop collaborative spirit
Divide labor on substantive areas and technical tasks
Allow sufficient time for final editing
Develop project guidelines to ensure site continuity
Prepare for new grading/assessment schemes
