Social Security Number Change Information

You have asked to change your social security number in the University's Human Resources System. To ensure proper tax reporting, federal regulations require employers to validate that each employee's social security number and name on the employer's records exactly matches the name which appears on the individual's social security card. Thus, in order to change either your social security number or your name in your personnel and payroll records, you must present your social security card which reflects the correct social security number to the Payroll Office in Room 212 of the Central Administration Building. Please call 412-456-9082 if you have questions or concerns.
