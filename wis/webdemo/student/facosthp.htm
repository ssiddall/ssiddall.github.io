Financial Aid Estimated Cost of Attendance Help

The cost of attendance is the total amount it should cost you to go to school. This amount includes tuition, room and board, fees, books and supplies, transportation, and personal expenses. These costs are affected by the financial aid year and budget period (full academic year, fall semester, spring semester, summer semester, etc.) in which you are enrolled. Other factors such as whether you are an undergraduate or graduate student, reside on or off-campus, or are a state resident or non-resident, determine your budget group classification.

Select an aid year, budget period, and budget characteristic from the appropriate pull-down lists, and press the Estimate button. An estimate of your Cost of Attendance displays based on these selections.
