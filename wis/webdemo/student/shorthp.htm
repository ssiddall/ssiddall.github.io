Short Term Credit Help

Short term loans are sometimes used to provide you with sufficient funds to handle emergency situations. You do not have to receive financial aid in order to receive short term credit. However, if you do receive financial aid, your unmet need or other financial data has no effect on your eligibility for short term credit.

The Account field identifies the funding source of the loan. You can have more than one loan from the same account or from different accounts.
