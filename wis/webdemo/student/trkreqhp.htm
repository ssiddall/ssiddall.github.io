Tracking Requirements Help

Depending upon the type of financial aid, a standard set of application requirements may be required of you before you can obtain the aid you are qualified to receive. For example, if you are scheduled to receive a Stafford Loan, a requirement of the loan program mandates that you meet with a financial aid administrator to review the terms of the loan before the loan is applied to your account. Outstanding obligations such as this are listed under the Unsatisfied Requirements heading. The Satisfied Requirements section lists those tracking requirements that you have already fulfilled.

The Tracking Messages link allows you to review any messages pertaining to these application requirements. These messages should provide you with more detail on how to satisfy the existing requirements. The Additional Requirements link displays those requirements that are specific to a term, rather than the entire year; it may also list those requirements that are not part of a standard educational aid program.
