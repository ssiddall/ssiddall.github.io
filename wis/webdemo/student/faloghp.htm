Student Login Help

You must enter your Student ID and your Personal Identification Number (PIN) to gain access to the Web Financial Aid Information System. If you have forgotten your ID or your PIN, please contact the Financial Aid or Registrar's Office either in person or via email to get your ID or PIN. You will not be granted access to the system if you are not eligible to apply, or if you are not an active student.
