General Financial Aid Information Help

Select one of the General Financial Aid Information options.

The Financial Aid Availability option enables you to review information regarding the various types of financial aid available to you. You can read about grants, loans, scholarships, and work study programs. There is information concerning the federal, state, and institutional sources of financial aid. You can also use the links on this page to request applications and additional information for many of these programs.

The Financial Aid Cost of Attendance option allows you to estimate the costs associated with attending this school based on the financial aid year, budget period, and budget characteristics.

The Financial Application and Information Links option provides you with the means to apply for financial aid. This option also allows you to access several other financial aid world wide web sites.

The E-mail SCT University Financial Aid Office option enables you to send electronic mail directly to SCT University.
