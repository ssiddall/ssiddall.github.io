Applicant/Student Financial Aid Access Menu Help

If you would like to review the status of your financial aid package, select one of the Financial Aid Information Request options. The Overall Status of Financial Aid option allows you to review the status of your financial aid application, tracking requirements, budgeted cost of attendance, financial award package, other resources, academic history, satisfactory academic progress, financial aid holds, short term credit, credits to your student account, financial aid history, and any defaults on loans. Other Financial Aid Information Request options include financial aid history, tracking requirements, other resources, current award package, short term credit, applicant messages and a means to respond to these messages, student employment, financial aid payments, and the ability to draft an electronic mail message.

Since all of these statuses are determined by a defined aid year, you can change this parameter to match your needs with the Select Parameters option.
