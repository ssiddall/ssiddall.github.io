Financial Aid Information Access Help

Select one of the Financial Aid Information Access options.

The General Financial Information option enables you to access information regarding financial aid availability (types of aid and sources of aid), provides a means of estimating your costs associated with attending this school, and includes financial aid application and information links.

The Applicant/Student Financial Aid Access menu allows you to view the various elements of your financial aid package. The options included on this menu include the overall status of financial aid, financial aid history, tracking requirements, other resources, current award package, short term credit, applicant messages, student employment, and financial aid payments.
