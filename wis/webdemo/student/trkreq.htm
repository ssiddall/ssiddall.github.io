The requirements that you must fulfill in order to receive a specific form of aid are listed beneath the Unsatified Requirements heading. Any previously outstanding requirements that you have already satisfied display under the Satisfied Requirements heading. You can also select the Tracking Messages link to review messages pertaining to these requirements. The Additional Requirements link displays those requirements that are specific to a term, rather than the entire year; it may also list those requirements that are not part of a standard educational aid program.
