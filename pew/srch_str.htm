Strategies for Searching Online Databases


Building Block Method

The most commonly used strategy for online database searching.

  • Identify the concepts and select the terms to represent each concept.

Combine the terms with Boolean OR.

  • Combine the concepts with Boolean operators, most often AND.


Successive Fractions
