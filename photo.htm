Photography

For several years, I worked as a photographer doing industrial photography, creating and managing a five-person industrial processing lab for a direct marketing company, and filming weddings. (In about five years, I covered nearly 40 weddings, which is about 39 too many.) Needless to say, I much prefer non-commercial photography, and perhaps as compensation for the time spent trying to make money, I prefer to film natural textures. I've also pickled my fair share of silver on a scanning electron microscope.
