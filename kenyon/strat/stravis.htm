Strategic Vision for Information Resources



This Three Year Plan for Information Technology is a working document, always in transition, always in need of revision. It is important to note, however, that strategic plans do not succeed unless they are built upon a strategic vision shared by the stakeholders. Kenyon College's strategic vision for information resources is not articulated in this document; indeed, it may never have been drafted, discussed and recorded. The newly-formed Information Resources Council has on its agenda the task of helping the campus develop this shared vision.
