The Video Conferencing Cookbook

The Video Conferencing Cookbook is accessible at the ViDe (Video Development Initiative) site. This comprehensive web site has been built by member institutions of the Southeastern Universities Research Association. The Cookbook provides an overview of videoconferencing applications, a glossary of terms, and practical steps for setting up systems. Highly recommended background reading.
