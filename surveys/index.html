This survey is no longer open for submissions

 

Thank you for taking the time to come and participate in this survey.

 

There are three pages to this survey and at the bottom of each page, there will be a link to the next page. Pre-testing shows that this survey takes no more than 10 to 15 minutes.

 

We need your candid responses to this survey. Please don't "overanalyze" the questions - your first impulse is probably your best response for this study. When going through this document, try to imagine yourself saying your responses. Rate your response on a scale from strongly agreeing to strongly disagreeing, based on how comfortable you are with each statement.

 

No information that can identify you will be logged in this survey. Answer freely. Only aggregate responses to quantitative data will be used. There will be no way to find out who answered the survey questions.

 

Thank you very much for taking this survey, and good luck on your exams.

 

Scott Siddall, Program Director
